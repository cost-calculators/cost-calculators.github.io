Definition

Shopify Plus ROI is the net financial benefit of upgrading to Plus — the extra revenue from Plus capabilities minus the higher platform fee.

What is the Shopify Plus ROI calculator?

This free tool helps you decide whether moving to Shopify Plus makes financial sense. It weighs the extra revenue Plus can unlock against its higher monthly cost, so the upgrade decision is grounded in numbers rather than feature lists.

How this calculator works

It applies your expected uplift to current revenue to estimate extra income, subtracts the difference between Plus and your current platform cost, and annualises the result. It also shows the break-even uplift — the smallest improvement that makes Plus pay for itself.

How to read your result

The headline is the net annual benefit. If it is comfortably positive, Plus likely pays off; if it is near zero or negative, you may be early. The break-even uplift tells you how small an improvement Plus needs to deliver to justify itself at your revenue.

When should you talk to a partner?

The real value of Plus depends on which features you would actually use — checkout extensibility, scripts, automation, B2B. A Plus partner can map those to your business and firm up the uplift estimate. Talk to our Shopify Plus team.

Is Shopify Plus worth it?

"Is Shopify Plus worth it" is one of the most searched questions in ecommerce, and the Shopify Plus ROI comes down to maths. Shopify Plus costs roughly $2,300+/month, so the upgrade pays off when the extra revenue its features unlock — checkout extensibility, scripts and automation, higher API limits, and B2B — exceeds that incremental cost. At higher revenue, a small percentage uplift easily covers the fee; at lower revenue, the Shopify Plus ROI is tighter, which this calculator makes explicit through the break-even uplift figure.

Remember that Plus value is not purely revenue. Automation that removes manual work, faster checkout, and B2B capabilities all contribute to the real return on Shopify Plus, even when they are harder to put in a spreadsheet.

Getting the most from Shopify Plus

To maximise your Shopify Plus ROI, identify the specific Plus-only features that solve a real constraint in your business before upgrading — a customised checkout, wholesale channel, or automation you cannot build on standard Shopify. The brands that see the strongest Shopify Plus ROI adopt these capabilities deliberately rather than upgrading for the badge. A Plus partner can map features to outcomes so the upgrade is justified by results.

Frequently asked questions

Is Shopify Plus worth it?

It depends on your revenue and which Plus features you would use. At higher revenue a small uplift covers the cost easily; at lower revenue the case is tighter. This calculator shows your net benefit and break-even point.

How much does Shopify Plus cost?

Plus is a higher monthly platform fee than standard Shopify, often quoted around a few thousand dollars per month depending on contract and revenue. Confirm current pricing with Shopify, and enter your figure in the calculator.

What do you get with Shopify Plus?

Plus adds checkout extensibility, scripts and automation, higher API limits, wholesale/B2B tools, multiple expansion stores, and more. The financial case rests on actually using these.

What uplift should I expect from Plus?

There is no fixed number — it comes from the specific features you adopt. Use a conservative figure tied to concrete improvements like a customised checkout or automated workflows.

When is it too early for Plus?

If the net annual benefit is near zero or negative at your revenue, you may be better staying on standard Shopify and revisiting as you grow or as a specific Plus feature becomes essential.

Is this estimate exact?

No. It is a planning estimate based on your inputs and an assumed uplift. A Plus specialist can refine it against your actual use cases.
